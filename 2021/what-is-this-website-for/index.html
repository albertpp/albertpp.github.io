#0. Why building this website?

I’m always a lover of telling, no matter what I tell–stories, my feelings, daily routines, magics, legends–I just tell them all. So when I grow up to be a college student, with all the staff I learn from textbook, my teachers and sometimes from websites, I can’t suppress my urge to share them with people. I myself own a Wechat Subscriber named “Environmental Science and Policy” (of course the real name is in Chinese instead of English), where I post mostly my literature reading, my views on issues concerned with environmental science and policy, mostly policy. And from time to time, I write some technical articles sharing what methods can be used in environmental research, like machine learning in building air modeling, drawing scientific graphs with R and Python, etc.

Then there comes the reason why I build this website. First, articles I post on my Subscriber is in Chinese, so I will post English version on my blog. Second, there can be some difficulties if I want to share blocks of codes on Wechat, while the website is an appropriate place.

#1.For literature sharing

Every week I carefully read about 5-10 journal articles and I will choose two of them to share my notes and my opinions for everyone to discuss. The Chinese version will go first, and in 2 days will English version be published on this website. And I will occasionally conclude a series of articles that share a same theme or use similar methods or show contradictory results.

#2.For showing myself

Many people may wonder why I have to build an English website. Of course one of the reasons is that I will pursue my study aboard that I must know how to advertise myself in English. On this website you can find introduction to my research work, my profile and even my personal life. Please feel free to contact me via my emails.

#3. For code writing

Like I said first, there are sometimes when Wechat Subscriber is not that convenient–code writing, for example. Therefore when I finish a special series concerned with code sharing, I will briefly talk about them in Wechat and post the detailed information on this website and my Github.

 

Hope I can really keep on well in this task and I’m ready for challenges!
