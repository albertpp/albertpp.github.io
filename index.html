Hi, I'm Yuang Chen (陈宇昂), a senior student at Peking University (PKU) , College of Urban and Environmental Sciences where I'm majoring in Environmental Science and have a minor in Economics from National School of Development.

I work in the energy-environment-economy (3E), ranging from pollutant transmission to energy modelling and policies. At PKU, I've been a RA to Prof. Xuejun Wang for three years, working on the transmission and related policy research about ammonia, nitrogen and mercury.

Besides, I have some outside collaborations with Prof. Valerie Karplus from Carnegie Mellon University on energy policy research, with Prof. Gang He from Stony Brook on energy modelling, and with Prof. Julie Zimmerman from Yale University on industrial ecology. See details in "Projects" section.

Rapid increase in China’s industrial ammonia emissions: evidence from unit-based mapping

Yuang Chen, Qianru Zhang, Xingrui Cai, Haoran Zhang, Huiming Lin, Chaoyue Zheng, Zhanqiang Guo, Shanying Hu, Long Chen, Shu Tao, Maodian Liu, Xuejun Wang
Environmental Science & Technology [link] [pdf]

We established an unprecedented high-spatial-resolution dataset of China’s industrial NH3 emission using up-to-date measurements of NH3 and point source-level information covering eight major industries and 27 subdivided processes.
